Welcome to Henry Ty's blessed collection of projects!

I've had experience with Javascript since 2017 (using p5.js) and started putting stuff on this website in 2019. We've got a wide variety of things to play with like games, simulations, and AI. Enjoy!




Games: Things to play with

Word wizard: a remake of an old Flash game called Anagram Magic. After Flash died, my sisters asked me to re-create it.

Purgatory: I was following along with a tutorial on Raycasting and this came out. I genuinely don't remember making this.

Snake Re-remastered: A classic arcade game made in 2020 and again in 2023.

The Maze: Literally the first thing added to this website back in 2020. For posterity's sake (and not laziness), I have kept it untouched.




Sims: Things to look at

The Gallery of Multidimensional Surfaces: A collection of 3-D and 4-D objects. Stare at them and be mesmerized.

The Sorting Collection: A bunch of ways to arrange a list of rectangles in order. Look at them. Watch the sorting. Experience the hypnotic beauty of sorting.

The Hall of Fractals: Just a collection of fractals. Bear witness the simplistic beauty of infinite fractals.

TJSVMTDHAA: A virtual machine running entirely in the webpage. The biggest thing I've made




AI: Making things do things.

Tic-Tac-Toe: Optimal play with a brute-force search.

Genetic ants: Janky genetic algorithm that I made instead of doing homework.

Perceptron: The simplest problem for my neural network library (made from scratch!)

Cartpole: Teaching a neural network to balance a stick on its end using Q-learning
