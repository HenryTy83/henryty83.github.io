Welcome to Hex-Connect!

Think "Wordle" (but there's 16 words so more like Hexaderdle) meets "Connections":

Use the keyboard (or your keyboard) to guess what the 16 words are. Click on a square to place your focus your guesses on that particular word. Like with Wordle, green means good, yellow means right letter, wrong place, and gray means wrong letter.

Your guess can be of any length and doesn't even have to be a word.

Once you get all 16 words. It's just like the Connections; find the make 4 categories, each with 4 words them.

You're smart. I'm sure you can figure it out...
